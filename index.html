DFZ

Dance Club IIT BHU

Popularly known as DFz (Dance Freakz), the doors of the club are open for anyone who is willing to move his body a bit to the rhythm. Focusing on versatility, we perform various dance forms like Hip-Hop, Bollywood, Salsa, Folk Dance, Classical, Freestyle, Contemporary, Krumping, House, and many more. The Dance Club organizes expert and beginner-level workshops for students of the Institute throughout the year.
